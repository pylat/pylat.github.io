About me: I received my PhD in July 2020 jointly from KU Leuven (Belgium) and IMT Lucca (Italy). My research has focused on the development of proximal algorithms for large-scale and distributed optimization, and their applications in machine learning and control. My PhD thesis can be found here.

Research interests: nonsmooth (non)convex optimization, minimax problems, operator splitting techniques, block-coordinate and incremental methods.
