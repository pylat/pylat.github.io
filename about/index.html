About me

I received my PhD in July 2020 jointly from KU Leuven (Belgium) and IMT Lucca (Italy), under the supervision of Prof. Panagiotis Patrinos and Prof. Alberto Bemporad. My PhD thesis can be found here.

Currently my research focuses on nonsmooth and (non)-convex optimization problems, and their applications to machine learning and signal processing.
